Nutrients in Vegetables and Fruits

Vegetables and fruits are packed with essential nutrients that help the body function at its best. Here are some key nutrients:

  • Fiber: Important for digestive health and maintaining healthy blood sugar levels.
  • Potassium: A mineral that helps regulate fluid balance and supports heart health.
  • Antioxidants: Help fight free radicals and reduce inflammation in the body.

Vitamins in Vegetables and Fruits

Vitamins are essential for various bodily functions, and different fruits and vegetables offer a range of vitamins. Some key vitamins include:

  • Vitamin A: Important for vision and immune health. Found in carrots, sweet potatoes, and spinach.
  • Vitamin C: Boosts immunity and supports skin health. Found in oranges, strawberries, and bell peppers.
  • Vitamin K: Plays a role in blood clotting and bone health. Found in kale, spinach, and broccoli.
